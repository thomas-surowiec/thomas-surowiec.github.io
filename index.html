I previously worked as a professor (W2) at Philipps-Universität Marburg in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science from 2016 to 2022, where I lead the research group on optimization. Prior to that I was an assistant professor (W1) at Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin in the Department of Mathematics from 2014 to 2016, where I lead the research group on nonsmooth optimization and variational analysis.

My research is in the development of theory and algorithms in mathematical optimization.

Research

My research is in the development of theory and algorithms in mathematical optimization. More specifically, I am interested in PDE-constrained optimization under uncertainty, nonsmooth optimization and optimal control of partial differential equations. My work makes use of a wide array of techniques from functional and variational analysis, PDE theory, statistics, and management science.
